HOW TO STAY SAFE FROM COVID -19

Made By Prograd Junior

Wash your hands regularly

Regular hand wash with soap and sanitiser for 30 seconds will help avoid germs

Cover your mouth and nose

Cover your mouth and nose while sneezing or coughing in public

Stay Home Stay Safe

Avoid Travelling or being in crowded Places. Prefer Stay indoors

Eat Healthy

Avoid consuming junk food.Eat as healthy as possible to boost your immunity

Stay Calm

Regular exercise or meditation is a great way to keep yourself calm and composed

BE AWARE ,BE SAFE
